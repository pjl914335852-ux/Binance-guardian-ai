## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Get the Bot**
   - Search for `@heiovdh_bot` on Telegram
   - Or scan the QR code in our README

2. **Start Chatting**
   - Send `/start` to begin
   - Ask questions in plain language
   - Or use voice messages (if enabled)

3. **Check a Coin**
   - Type the coin name (e.g., "BTC")
   - Or click "🛡️ Check Coin" button
   - Get instant safety assessment

4. **Learn Daily**
   - Click "📚 Today's Lesson" for safety tips
   - Click "📖 Today's Case" for real scam stories
   - Build your crypto safety knowledge
